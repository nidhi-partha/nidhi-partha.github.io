About Me

I am a senior at Lynbrook High School in California and interested in technology and its applications to helping people and making a difference. I have worked on several projects using AI for health and have published and presented my work at conferences.

Recently, I have been researching AI for education, working on a web-app (AI-TA), to more broadly disseminate AI knowledge and ethics, using AI for customization. I have been expanding on this work at Stanford, building an AI tutor for journalism.

I have been recognized by the NCWIT National Aspirations in Computing award three times, and am also a three-time presidential service gold award winner. Outside of school, I am passionate about dance and volunteering and am also a blogger.
